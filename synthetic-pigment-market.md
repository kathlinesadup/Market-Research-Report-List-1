<p><p>The synthetic pigment market is primarily categorized into organic and inorganic pigments. Organic pigments are derived from carbon compounds, known for their vivid colors, excellent transparency, and high tinting strength, making them popular in applications like paints and inks. In contrast, inorganic pigments are mineral-based, characterized by their durability and opacity. They often exhibit excellent lightfastness and chemical stability, making them suitable for coatings and plastics. Both types play essential roles in various industries, contributing to color and performance.</p></p>
