<p><p>Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT) Oil is derived from coconut oil or palm kernel oil and is primarily categorized into three types based on chain length: C6 (caproic acid), C8 (caprylic acid), C10 (capric acid), and C12 (lauric acid). C6 and C8 are rapidly metabolized for energy, popular in ketogenic diets, while C10 and C12 offer different health benefits. The market for these oils is growing due to their applications in dietary supplements, food, and cosmetics, driven by rising health awareness.</p></p>
