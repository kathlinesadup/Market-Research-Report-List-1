<p><p>The passenger vehicle starter and alternator market is segmented into two main categories: alternators and starter motors. Alternators are responsible for generating electrical power while the engine is running, charging the battery and powering vehicle systems. Starter motors, on the other hand, initiate the engine's operation by converting electrical energy from the battery into mechanical energy. Both components are essential for vehicle functionality, ensuring reliable starting and continuous electrical supply, thus driving advancements in automotive technology and increasing market demand.</p></p>
