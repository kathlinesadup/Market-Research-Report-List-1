<p><strong>The Boat Trim System (BTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boat Trim System (BTS) market focuses on technologies that enhance a vessel's performance, stability, and fuel efficiency. Key market types include trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces mounted on the transom that optimize the boat’s angle in the water, improving lift and reducing drag. Interceptors, on the other hand, are fixed plates that alter the water flow beneath the hull, providing rapid response adjustments for better control and handling at various speeds.</p></p>
