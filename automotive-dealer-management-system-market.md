<p><p>The Automotive Dealer Management System (DMS) market is categorized into two main types: on-site and cloud-based solutions. On-site DMS involves software installed directly on dealership servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based DMS provides remote access via the internet, ensuring flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. This model allows dealers to access real-time data from anywhere, facilitating easier updates and reduced IT burden, making it increasingly popular among modern dealerships.</p></p>
