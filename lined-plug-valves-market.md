<p><p>The lined plug valves market is categorized into several types based on the lining materials used. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) lined valves offer excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature capability, making them suitable for demanding applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) lined valves are known for their exceptional corrosion resistance and low friction properties. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) lined valves provide good chemical resistance and transparency for monitoring flow. Other categories may include various specialized lining materials catering to specific industrial needs, enhancing performance and durability.</p></p>
