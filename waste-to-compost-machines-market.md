<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waste to compost machines are categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic types based on their operational capabilities. Fully automatic machines handle the entire composting process autonomously, from shredding waste to managing temperature and aeration, requiring minimal human intervention. In contrast, semi-automatic machines necessitate some manual input, such as loading materials and monitoring the process, while still automating critical functions like mixing and ventilation. Both types cater to varying user needs and scales of composting operations, promoting efficient waste management.</p></p>
